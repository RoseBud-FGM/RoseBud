RoseBud

RoseBud is a comprehensive resource designed to combat Female Genital Mutilation/Cutting (FGM/C) by promoting awareness, reporting, and counseling services. The user-friendly interface, confidential reporting channels, and interactive forum provide a safe and inclusive space for survivors, activists, and community members to connect and collaborate. With support for multiple languages and a community-driven approach, the app aims to empower individuals and communities to take action against FGM/C and work towards its eradication.
