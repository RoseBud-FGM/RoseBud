Get closer to RoseBud

RoseBud is a vital tool in the fight against female genital mutilation/cutting. By addressing the challenges faced by communities affected by this harmful practice and providing a platform for education, awareness-raising, and support, we can make a significant impact towards achieving a world free from harm and discrimination.

Together, let us take a stand and work towards ending FGM/C and promoting gender equality.

  • - A web-based application “RoseBud” to eliminate Female Genital Mutilation/Cutting (FGM/C).
  • - Features educational content, including videos, infographics, and FAQs.
  • - The application is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to all.
  • - To ensure that the application is effective and relevant to the users.
