What we provide

Our Services

A comprehensive platform for survivors and allies, offering counseling, reporting, forum, and tracking services.

Reporting
Forum
Counseling
Tracking

  • Reporting

    Our FGM/C project helps in reporting by providing safe and confidential channels for community members to report incidents of the practice, and by empowering girls and women with the knowledge and resources to speak out against FGM/C and seek support from relevant authorities.

    1500 Reports
    Report Something

  • Forum

    A forum can help by providing a safe space for community members to engage in open and honest discussions about FGM/C, share personal experiences and knowledge, and collaborate on strategies to prevent and respond to incidents of the practice. A forum can also help to build solidarity and promote community-driven solutions that prioritize the health and well-being of girls and women.

    3000 members
    Join The Forum!

  • Counseling

    We help in counseling by providing access to trained professionals who can offer psychological and emotional support to survivors and their families, and by promoting community-based approaches that prioritize the health and well-being of girls and women affected by FGM/C.

    120 Counselors
    Book a counselor

  • Tracking

    Tracking cases of FGM/C through our web application is crucial for several reasons. It allows us to monitor the prevalence of the practice in different regions and communities, which can inform targeted awareness campaigns and prevention efforts. It also provides survivors with a safe and confidential platform to report their experiences and seek support from trained counselors.

    150 Active cases
    Track your report
Call To Action

Join the Movement Today!

We must take action to end the harmful practice of FGM/C. This includes educating ourselves and others about the physical and psychological harms of the practice, promoting alternative cultural practices that value the rights and autonomy of girls and women, and reporting incidents to empower survivors and prevent future occurrences.
